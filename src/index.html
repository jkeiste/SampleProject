About Me

Hi! My name is Jon Keister, and I am actively studying to become a self-taught software developer. I am currently employed as a full time Sr IT Quality Analyst on a software development team within the HealthCare IT industry. The majority of my experience lies in manual and automated testing web applications developed within an ASP.NET development environment. I am interested in learning to code in order to develop a more analytical approach to problem solving, as well as improving communication with team members on a more technical level. I am learning to develop websites and web applications, ultimately with the goal being to advance my career. Much of my development knowledge, thusfar, has been acquired at TeamTreehouse, and SoloLearn. I find both of these websites to be excellent resources. In order to encourage myself and others to continue studying in the pursuit of betterment, I am going to start my personal journey with the now popular 100 days of coding challenge. Join me in this rewarding journey. You'll have fun, and learn along the way!
